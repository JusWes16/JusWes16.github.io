White Space and Clean Design

Apple Inc.

https://www.apple.com/

This image is a great example of using white space and keeping a clean design. Apple does a great job at making sure that they show enough information without overloading the screen with words and images. Apple uses a white background and a simple picture to portray a clean design and a pleasant viewing experience.

PARC: Alignment

Amazon.com Inc.

https://www.amazon.com/

Amazon.com is an example of textbook alignment. The way that this website is laid out makes it very easy to ready and follow. Each item on this home page is equal distance apart and lines up perfectly with the other items on the page. Doing this takes the stress off of the reader making it easy and simple to read and browse.
