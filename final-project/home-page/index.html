The Boston Temple

This is the Boston Massachusetts Temple. The Boston Temple was the 100th operating temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints.

This temple was the first temple to be built in New England and Massechusetts. The Boston Temple is one of the many temples where The Temple Inn & Suites is proud to be in operation. To learn more about the Boston Temple as well as other temples click here.
