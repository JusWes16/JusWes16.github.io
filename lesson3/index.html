Live Weather Report

The purpose of the Live Weather Report website is to inform the people in 3 specific towns of the current weather conditions that they are or might be experiencing. The hope is to make sure that the user has an easy experience location their specific weather condition. We want our site to serve as an informational platform to help our users more easily plan out and prepare for their day.
