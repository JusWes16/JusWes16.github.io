Target Audience

Our target audience with this website are first and foremost those living in the towns the site will cover. The audience could also be anyone looking to travel to or through one of the three towns. Our audience is anyone wanting to know more about the weather conditions in their town. Not only will this site allow our audience to check the current weather conditions but also future weather conditions for later that day or the upcoming week. Our target audience includes users between the ages of 13 and 70 years old. Our audience incoporates both men and women. Basically our target audience is anyone with a desire to know what the weather will be like in one of the three towns this site will cover.

Personas

  1. This user is a 25 year old female and newly married. She and her husband are looking at options for their Friday night date. They have 2 ideas, going to the theater or mini golfing. Before they make their decision, they want to take a look at what the weather is going to be like for Friday night. If the weather will be poor, they would choose to go to the theater. On the other hand, if the weather will be clear then they might choose to go mini golfing.
  2. This user is a 65 year old, retired male. He loves camping with his grandkids. He wants to take his 3 grandkids camping this weekend in the hills near one of the 3 towns covered by this site. They only want to go camping if the weather is going to be good. Before they decide to go, they will want to check what the weather is going to be like during the upcoming weekend.

Scenarios

  • A user might be looking at going camping during the weekend. Knowing what the weather might be could be the difference between actually going and staying home.
  • A user might be interested in attending an event one evening during the winter. In making their decition to attend or not they might want to check to see if it is supposed to snow or ice that night.
  • A user might be planning an outdoor party in their back yard. They would want to check the weather before commiting to having the event outdoors. If there is going to be bad weather then the user might want to move the party inside.
  • A user might be trying to figure out what they want to wear for the day. Before making the decision, the user might want to check what the weather might be like for the day. What the user chooses to wear could depend greatly on the weather for that day.
  • A user might want to go fishing one day. While planning his trip he will want to check the weather. If there is a forecast of thunderstorms or rain then he would most likely want to reschedule his trip.
