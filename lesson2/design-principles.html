PARC: Alignment

Amazon.com Inc.

https://www.amazon.com/

Amazon.com is an example of textbook alignment. The way that this website is laid out makes it very easy to ready and follow. Each item on this home page is equal distance apart and lines up perfectly with the other items on the page. Doing this takes the stress off of the reader making it easy and simple to read and browse.

Fitt's Law

Pandora Media Inc.

https://www.pandora.com/

Pandora's website is a great example of Fitt's Law. Fitt's Law simply puts states that bigger and closer something is, the easier it is to use. On Pandora's website, the play button is in a central location where it can be accessed easily. The play button is also noticeably distanced from the other buttons making it easier to press. All of this combined helps the user know where the button is and makes it easy to use.
